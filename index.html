The effect of lockdown on deaths

India had 4 lockdown in the year 2020 followed by the cooldown phases in 2021, this chart displays the effect of lockdown on the spread. There were many deaths during the lockdown period which were not virus caused such as suicides and hunger and migration.

Deaths caused due to oxygen shortage

India underwent a serious shortage of oxygen tanks and ICU beds as critical cases were on rise in 2021 as compared to non critical cases which did not need serious attention. This led to a lot of deaths and concern for the future. This line graph shows the surge of deaths which may directly be a result of the shortage.

Additional facts about Covid-19 in India

Vaccination

India became the world's largest vaccine manufacturer in 2021, however, despite this feat, only 15% of the population was vaccinated which still posed issues for the country's covid situation

Deaths

Despite having a high population and population density, India had a very low death ratio as compared to other countries, however this could be caused by less death reporting

Lockdown

India imposed a month long lockdown for its 1.3 billion population, which was the most far-reaching measure undertaken by any goverment to curb the spread

Oxygen

In 2021, India underwent a massive shortage of oxygen which led to multiple deaths as shown above, during this period, many countries such as UK and US offered help by sending oxygen tanks
