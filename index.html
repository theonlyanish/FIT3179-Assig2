India

Covid-19 in India took a heavy toll on the population and the wellbeing for a long period of time due to a number of factors such as population density, vaccinations, ICU beds, etc. This map shows the growth of covid per state in the form of heatmap using month scale on the axis below.

The effect of lockdown on deaths

India had 4 lockdown in the year 2020 followed by the cooldown phases in 2021, this chart displays the effect of lockdown on the spread. There were many deaths during the lockdown period which were not virus caused such as suicides and hunger and migration.

Deaths caused due to oxygen shortage

India underwent a serious shortage of oxygen tanks and ICU beds as critical cases were on rise in 2021 as compared to non critical cases which did not need serious attention. This led to a lot of deaths and concern for the future. This line graph shows the surge of deaths which may directly be a result of the shortage.
